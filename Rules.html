🏢 Govind Boys Hostel

Rules & Regulations

Please read and follow all rules carefully for a safe and respectful environment.

⏰ Entry Timing

Entry time is 10:00 PM. Late entry allowed only with parental confirmation.

📝 Leave Application

Going home requires a signed application from the warden.

📞 Parental Contact

Warden may contact parents at any time for verification or concerns.

👥 Visitor Policy

Visitors are allowed only with prior permission from the warden.

💳 Fee Payment

Pay fees by the 5th of every month to avoid penalties.

🔐 Security Refund

No refund of security deposit if you leave within one month.

🚫 Illegal Activities

Any illegal activities will lead to disciplinary/legal action.

⚡ Electrical Equipment

Extra devices need manager's approval. Only standard usage allowed.

💸 Property Damage

Damage caused will be deducted from the security deposit.

🚭 Substance Prohibited

Drugs, alcohol, and tobacco are strictly prohibited inside hostel.

🎒 Belongings

Hostel is not responsible for personal belongings. Keep them safe.

🚷 Restricted Entry

Outsiders/relatives are not allowed inside the hostel without approval.

🍽️ Meal Timings

Breakfast (7–9 AM), Lunch (12–2 PM), Dinner (7–9 PM).

📜 Rule Adherence

All residents must follow the rules. Violations will not be tolerated.
